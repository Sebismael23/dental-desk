## Answering Your Specific Questions

### Q: "Do they go to website.com and login?"

**Yes.** Each practice logs into your central app:
- URL: `app.dentaldeskai.com` (or whatever you name it)
- They use email/password to log in
- After login, they see ONLY their own data

### Q: "Do I have access to everything?"

**Yes, as the admin.** You have a separate admin panel that shows:
- All practices
- All calls across all practices
- Revenue/billing information
- System health metrics
- Ability to impersonate practices for support

### Q: "Can they only see their own data?"

**Yes, enforced by Row-Level Security.** Supabase RLS ensures that even if someone tries to hack the URL or API, they can only see data where `practice_id` matches their account.

### Q: "What's the very best architecture?"

**Multi-tenant SaaS with Row-Level Security.** This is the industry-standard pattern used by:
- Slack (each workspace is isolated)
- Notion (each workspace is isolated)
- Every modern B2B SaaS

It's secure, scalable, and maintainable.
